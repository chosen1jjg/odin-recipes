Sweet Potatoes

Just like candy

Southern Candied Yams bring that feel-good magic to the table. Imagine this: tender, sweet yams bathed in a sweet buttery glaze, with warm hints of cinnamon and vanilla filling the kitchen. I’m telling you, this dish doesn’t just sit on the side, it demands attention.

Ingredients

  • 1 large bag of sweet Potatoes
  • 1 pack of salted butter
  • Cinnamon to taste
  • 1/4 cup of nutmeg
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/8 cup of vanilla extract
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F.
  2. Peel and slice sweet potatoes into 1/2 inch pieces, then arrange them in a greased baking dish.
  3. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium-low heat and stir in sugar, spices, and vanilla until smooth
  4. Pour mixture over sweet potatoes, mix them up a little and cover with foil, and bake for 45 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to 425°F, remove the foil, and bake for another 15 minutes for a beautiful caramelized finish.
