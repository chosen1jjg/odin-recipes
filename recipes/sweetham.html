Sweet Ham

Ham me some more

The best sweet and tangy 5-ingredient glaze for a holiday worthy ham. This ham glaze is the most delicious combination of brown sugar, honey, Dijon mustard, and just a splash of fruity apple cider vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Brown sugar
  • 1 cup of Honey
  • 1/4 cup of Dijon Mustard
  • 1 pack of unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup of apple cider vinegar
  1. Wrap the ham in aluminum foil
  2. Begin baking the ham at 120°F.
  3. Make the glaze by simmering all the ingredients until thickened.
  4. Brush the glaze onto the ham after about 20 minutes.
  5. Finish baking ham for another 20 minutes at 130°F.
